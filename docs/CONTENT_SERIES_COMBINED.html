The Second Tower: Engagements

Quotes map to Engagement data.

  • High-volume
  • Timestamped

Modeling quotes as engagements keeps them lightweight and scalable. Get the foreign key wrong, and engagements orphan â€” they exist but can't be linked to identity, making them invisible to automation and agents.

Why This Matters for Agentforce

Here's where the modeling decision pays off.

When you ask an AI agent a question like:
